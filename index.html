Grok Prince is an original browser platformer. Graphics, audio, levels, and code are new. Gameplay and mood are inspired by a mix of classic titles — including Castlevania, Metroid / Metroidvania exploration, Prince of Persia–era cinematic platforming, and similar dungeon-and-sword adventures — not a remake or clone of any single game.
